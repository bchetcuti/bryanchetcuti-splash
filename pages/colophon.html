Colophon & Digital Trust

This site isn’t about promotion - it’s about presence. A single page that says: I stand behind what I build, and how I build it.

It exists to be small, fast, and durable. Hand-written in vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Served through Cloudflare Pages with HTTPS enforced and caching controlled. No frameworks. No cookies. No dependencies I can’t explain.

Typography is set in Space Grotesk and Inter - clear, modern, legible. Colours draw from a palette built for calm contrast: ink, paper, and the occasional accent of energy.

Design intent: minimal surface, maximal clarity.

Every line of code was written with the same philosophy I bring to my work in technology delivery and governance - trust through transparency, security through simplicity, and accountability through design. You can view the source, fork it, or read it line by line. There’s nothing hidden behind the curtain.

Privacy & data: This site collects nothing. No cookies, pixels, analytics, or logs that identify visitors. If you reach out to collaborate, it’s person-to-person - not profile-to-algorithm.

Resilience: Hosted on a redundant global edge network. Backed up offline. Tested for performance, accessibility, and integrity. Built to remain readable, even if everything else breaks.
