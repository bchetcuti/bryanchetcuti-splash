Colophon & Digital Trust

This site isn’t about promotion - it’s about presence.

A single page that says: I stand behind what I build, and how I build it.

It exists to be small, fast, and durable.
Hand-written in vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
Served via Cloudflare Pages with HTTPS enforced and caching controlled.
No frameworks. No cookies. No dependencies I can’t explain.

Typography: Space Grotesk and Inter
Colour palette: ink, paper, and accent
Design intent: minimal surface, maximal clarity.

Every line of code follows the same principles I bring to technology delivery and governance

  • Trust through transparency,
  • Security through simplicity, and
  • Accountability through design.
You can view the source, fork it, or read it line by line. There’s nothing hidden behind the curtain.

Privacy & Data:
This site collects nothing. No cookies, pixels, or tracking.
If you reach out to collaborate, it’s person-to-person - not profile-to-algorithm.

Resilience:

Backed up offline. Tested for performance, accessibility, and integrity.
Built to remain readable even if everything else breaks.
